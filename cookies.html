Cookie policy

We use cookies to keep things running smoothly — and we’re completely open about how.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and improve your experience. Think of them as tiny, helpful companions.

How we use cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies, divided into these categories:

  • Strictly necessary: essential for the site to work (e.g., navigation, security).
  • Preference: remember your settings and choices.
  • Analytics: anonymized data to help us understand which articles resonate most.
✧ Your choice matters
You can control or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or third‑party profiling.

Your consent

When you first visit our site, we ask for your consent to place non‑essential cookies. You can change your mind at any time by adjusting your browser settings or by contacting us. We respect your privacy, always.

Updates & contact

This Cookie Policy may be updated occasionally. The latest version is always available here. If you have any questions, please reach out — we’re happy to help.
