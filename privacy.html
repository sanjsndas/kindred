🔒 your privacy matters

Trust & transparency

We believe in clear, honest privacy practices. Here’s how we handle your data with care.

Our commitment

At Kindred Butter Grove, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We aim to be as transparent as a sunlit grove.

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal data: name, email address, and any details you provide via contact forms or subscriptions.
  • Usage data: anonymized analytics about how you interact with our site (pages visited, time spent, etc.).
  • Cookies & tracking: we use minimal cookies to enhance your experience — see our Cookie Policy for details.
✧ Your data, your control
You can opt out of non‑essential tracking at any time. We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Period.

How we use your information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and comments.
  • Send you occasional newsletters (only if you’ve opted in).
  • Improve our content and site performance through aggregate analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Our systems are regularly reviewed and updated.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, simply reach out to us — we’ll respond within 30 days.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the “last updated” date at the bottom.
